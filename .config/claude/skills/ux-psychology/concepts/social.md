# Social Influence

Leveraging trust and social credibility.

---

## Social Proof

**Definition**: Being influenced by others' opinions and actions.

**Implementation Guidelines**:

- Display reviews and ratings
- Show user counts
- Feature recognizable client logos

```jsx
// Social proof implementation
<TrustSection>
  <Stat>Trusted by 10,000+ companies</Stat>
  <ClientLogos>
    <Logo src="google.svg" />
    <Logo src="amazon.svg" />
    <Logo src="microsoft.svg" />
  </ClientLogos>
  <Reviews rating={4.8} count={2500} />
</TrustSection>
```

---

## Halo Effect

**Definition**: One positive trait elevates overall perception.

**Implementation Guidelines**:

- Display CSR activities
- Show awards and certifications
- Enhance brand image
